Directions

  • Enter the shape αα and the shape ββ.

  • To determine a percentile, enter the percentile (e.g. use 0.8 for the 80th percentile) in the pink box and select P(X<x)P(X<x) from the drop-down box and press "Tab" or "Enter" on your keyboard. The percentile xx will appear in the blue box.
